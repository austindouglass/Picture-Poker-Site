About

playpicturepoker.com is a simplified poker game based off of a minigame on the DS that I used to play as a kid. I created this site as a fun way to better my understanding of Javascript's connection with HTML and CSS while I look for work.

I plan to keep working on this site until it has enough content for people to continue playing on it for some time. Thank you for checking it out! :)
